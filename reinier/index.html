About Me

I’m a director, script consultant, screenwriter and script supervisor. In other words, I live and breathe filmmaking and storytelling.

For more than 12 years I’ve been working in the film and television industry for a broad range of productions from Hollywood, UK, France, South Africa and The Netherlands.

I love working on a film set, where you are part of an exhilarating creative process to visualise a compelling story. I’m passionate about the initial process of working on a screenplay, where ideas grow into a storyline and an incredible narrative comes to life.

Contact me if you are looking for a filmmaker or a creative professional to elevate your production.
